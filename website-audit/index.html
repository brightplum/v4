Is Your Website Costing You 20% More Than It Should?

Most non-profits overpay for website maintenance while missing opportunities to increase donations. Our free audit shows you how to fix both.

Your website is one of your largest operational expenses. But here's what most non-profits don't realize:

Poor website management costs you in two ways:

  1. You're paying 20% more than you should for maintenance
  2. You're losing donation opportunities every day

Most maintenance vendors keep your site running. But they don't:

  • Optimize your donation forms for higher conversion
  • Test different calls-to-action to increase giving

The Hidden Cost of Basic Maintenance

Your current vendor charges you for updates, patches, and fixes. But they're missing opportunities that could increase your donations by 15-30%:

Donation Form Optimization

Small changes to form design can increase completion rates dramatically. Are you testing different layouts, suggested giving amounts, and monthly vs. one-time options?

Mobile Giving Experience

60% of donors give on mobile devices. Is your donation experience optimized for phones and tablets, or are you losing gifts to frustration?

Donor Journey Mapping

Do you know where potential donors drop off? Which pages lead to donations and which ones cause people to leave?

A/B Testing

Are you testing different calls-to-action, headlines, and page layouts to see what drives more giving?

The Three Gaps Most Maintenance Vendors Leave

Gap 1: No Testing = Donation Form Failures

Cheap maintenance vendors apply updates without testing your donation forms. They don't know if they broke your payment processor integration until a donor reports it—or worse, until you notice donations have dropped.

What you need: Automated testing that verifies your donation forms work after every update.

Gap 2: No Strategy = Missed Revenue

Your maintenance vendor keeps the lights on. But are they:

  • Tracking which pages lead to donations?
  • Testing different donation form designs?
  • Optimizing your site for donor search terms?
  • Making recommendations that increase giving?

What you need: Strategic oversight that turns your website into a fundraising tool, not just an expense.

Gap 3: No CMS Optimization = Wasted Staff Time

Your communications team shouldn't need a developer to publish an impact story. Your fundraising team shouldn't struggle to update campaign pages.

What you need: A vendor who makes your CMS easy for your team to use, freeing up time for mission work.
